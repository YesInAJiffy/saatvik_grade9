# All Students Take Calculus
- Purpose: Helps determine the sign (+ or -) of trig functions in each quadrant.
- How it Works (Counter-clockwise from Quadrant I):
  - All: All functions (Sin, Cos, Tan) are positive. <br/>
  - Sine: Sine (and its reciprocal, Cosecant) are positive. <br/>
  - Tangent: Tangent (and Cotangent) are positive. <br/>
  - Cosine: Cosine (and Secant) are positive.  <br/>
